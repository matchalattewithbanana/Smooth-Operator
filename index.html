Audio Filters
17 real-time DSP audio filters processed by Lavalink — applied instantly with no quality loss
17 filters · 1 command
Filter Command
-filter
aliases: f, fx
Apply an audio filter by name. Use -filter list to see all filters. Use -filter clear to remove all filters.
-filter <name|list|clear>
Available Filters
-filter bassboost
Heavy bass boost via 10-band equalizer. Bumps the low end significantly.
-filter deepbass
Extreme sub-bass boost. Even heavier low-end than bassboost.
-filter nightcore
Speed and pitch increased to 1.3× — the classic nightcore effect.
-filter daycore
Speed and pitch decreased to 0.75× — the reverse of nightcore (slowed + reverb vibe).
-filter vaporwave
Speed and pitch at 0.8× for that nostalgic vaporwave aesthetic.
-filter slowed
Slows playback to 0.85× speed without changing pitch.
-filter chipmunk
High pitch + speed — chipmunk/alvin voice effect.
-filter darthvader
Pitch dropped to 0.5× — deep Darth Vader voice effect.
-filter lofi
Combined low-pass filter + EQ boost + slight slowdown for that lo-fi hip hop feel.
-filter 8d
Rotating stereo panning at 0.2Hz — audio feels like it's moving around your head.
-filter karaoke
Removes the center channel to suppress vocals — karaoke mode.
-filter tremolo
Tremolo effect — rapid volume oscillation at 2Hz.
-filter vibrato
Vibrato effect — pitch oscillation at 2Hz.
-filter vibration
Stronger vibrato at 4Hz — intense pitch wobble effect.
-filter soft
Low-pass smoothing filter — removes harsh high frequencies for a warmer sound.
-filter pop
Mid-range EQ boost tuned for pop music — enhances vocals and clarity.
-filter treble
High-frequency EQ boost — adds brightness and air to the sound.

DJ System
Full DJ role and permission system — restrict commands per-server, per-user, or server-wide
8 commands · Manage Server required
DJ Commands
-djsetup
aliases: djs
Enable DJ mode and set the DJ role. When enabled, all music commands require the DJ role (admins bypass).
Manage Server-djsetup @role | -djsetup toggle
-djinfo
aliases: dji
Show all current DJ settings: DJ role, DJ mode status, and list of restricted commands.
Manage Server-djinfo
-djreset
Reset all DJ settings to default — clears DJ role, disables DJ mode, removes all restrictions.
Manage Server-djreset
-djrestrict
aliases: restrictcommand
Toggle any individual command to be DJ-only, regardless of the global DJ mode setting.
Manage Server-djrestrict <command name>
-settings djrole
Set or clear the DJ role directly via settings. Members with this role can use DJ-restricted commands.
Manage Server-settings djrole @role | -settings djrole clear
